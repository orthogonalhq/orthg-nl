## Color Palette

### Backgrounds
- **Primary:** Black (`#000000` or near-black)
- **Cards:** Dark gray (subtle, ~`#141414`), rounded corners, subtle border

### Text
- **Primary:** White (`#FFFFFF`)
- **Secondary/Muted:** Medium gray (~`#888888`) — used for subtitles, taglines
- **Emphasis:** White bold — used inline for key statements

### Accent
- **Red:** Warm red/coral (~`#CC3333`) — used for card labels, feature titles (Build, Delegate, Improve, etc.)

### Imagery
- Dominant palette: Deep orange, molten red, amber glow
- Motifs: Glowing orbs, concentric rings, portals, volcanic/cosmic landscapes
- Reflective surfaces (water-like reflections beneath subjects)
- Rounded corners on images with subtle gray border/frame

## Layout Patterns *(from deck — adapt for web)*

### Two-Column Hero
- Left: 2x2 card grid or large image
- Right: Subtitle (muted) + large mono header + body text

### Feature Cards
- Dark background, rounded corners
- Red accent title (Fira Code)
- Thin horizontal separator line below title
- Body text in Geist, light gray

### Footer Bar
- Date tag (mono, bordered pill) | Logomark + label pill | Slogan (muted, right-aligned)
